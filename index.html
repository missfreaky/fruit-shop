About Us

Kiwifruit or Chinese gooseberry, is the edible berry of several species of woody vines in the genus Actinidia. The most common cultivar group of kiwifruit is oval, about the size of a large hen's egg: 5–8 centimetres in length and 4.5–5.5 cm in diameter. Kiwi is a fruit that is rich in nutrients, has a low calorie count, and is well-known for its high vitamin C content. It is also an excellent source of dietary fibre. Kiwi fruit nutrition includes minerals such as potassium, magnesium, vitamin C, vitamin A, vitamin E, and vitamin K.
